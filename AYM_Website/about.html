About Adventist Youth Ministries

Empowering young people to serve God and community through faith, fellowship, and service

Our Mission

The Adventist Youth Ministries (AYM) is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of young people aged 12-30. We provide opportunities for worship, fellowship, service, and leadership development within the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

Our Vision

To see every young person connected to Jesus Christ and actively involved in His mission to save the world. We envision a generation of young people who are passionate about God, committed to service, and equipped for leadership.

Our History

Over a century of empowering young people in the Seventh-day Adventist Church

Founded in 1907

The Adventist Youth Ministries was officially established in 1907, making it one of the oldest youth organizations in the world. Since its inception, AYM has been committed to developing young people spiritually, socially, and intellectually.

Global Impact

Today, AYM operates in over 200 countries and territories worldwide, serving millions of young people through various programs including Adventurer Clubs, Pathfinder Clubs, and Ambassadors for Christ.
