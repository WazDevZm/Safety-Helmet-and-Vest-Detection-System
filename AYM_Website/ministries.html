Our Ministries

Diverse opportunities for spiritual growth, leadership development, and community service

Adventurer Club

Ages 4-9

The Adventurer Club is designed to strengthen parent-child relationships for kids ages 4-9 by providing specialized weekly activities tuned into the psychological needs of this age group while simultaneously giving parents opportunities for learning parenting skills and enjoying special activities with their children.

  • Age-appropriate activities
  • Character development
  • Parent-child bonding
  • Fun learning experiences
Learn More

Pathfinder Club

Ages 10-15

Pathfinders are a worldwide organization of young people sponsored by the Seventh-day Adventist Church. Young people of any religious persuasion, or none at all, are welcome and encouraged to join the organization.

  • Outdoor adventures
  • Skill development
  • Leadership training
  • Community service
Learn More

Ambassadors for Christ

Ages 16-30

Ambassadors for Christ (AY) is a global youth movement that focuses on evangelism, discipleship, and leadership development. AY provides opportunities for young people to grow in their faith while serving their communities.

  • Evangelism training
  • Discipleship programs
  • Mission opportunities
  • Leadership development
Learn More

PCM (Pathfinder Club Ministry)

All Ages

PCM is a comprehensive ministry that supports and enhances the Pathfinder program through specialized training, resources, and leadership development for club leaders and staff.

  • Leader training
  • Resource development
  • Program support
  • Mentorship opportunities
Learn More

Master Guide Club

Ages 16+ (Adult Leaders)

The Master Guide Club is for dedicated adult leaders who have completed their Master Guide requirements and are committed to mentoring young people in their spiritual and character development.

  • Advanced leadership training
  • Mentorship programs
  • Specialized certifications
  • International service opportunities
  • Youth ministry specialization
