About AYM

Empowering young people to serve God and community

Our Mission

The Adventist Youth Ministries (AYM) is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of young people aged 12-30. We provide opportunities for worship, fellowship, service, and leadership development within the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

Our Vision

To see every young person connected to Jesus Christ and actively involved in His mission to save the world.
